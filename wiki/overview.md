# NASCAR DFS Racing Dynamics Overview

The objective of this vault is to persist lessons learned from the Gemini Antigravity IDE and Galaxie/ORLY simulators.

**Core Philosophies Tracked:**
1. **The Closer / Fader Dynamic**: Identifying which drivers reliably fade on the long run, and which drivers advance.
2. **Dominator Cash Overrides**: Strategies on how points are distributed by the leaders on specific track types (e.g. short tracks vs superspeedways).
3. **The 750HP Mental Model**: A specific framework focusing on the dynamic parameters surrounding high-HP, low-downforce setups.

This wiki grows organically as we ingest `race-synths` after each weekend's race.
