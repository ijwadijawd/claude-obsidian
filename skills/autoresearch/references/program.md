# NASCAR DFS AutoResearch Program

## Objectives
- Research NASCAR track histories, tire wear characteristics, and 750HP package updates.
- Deep dive into DFS optimization literature, betting strategies, and simulation methodologies.
- Analyze specific driver tendencies (Closers vs Faders).

## Confidene Scoring Requirements
- Academic papers (e.g. from ORLY or Galaxie framework equivalents): Baseline 0.9 confidence.
- Official NASCAR rules / telemetry stats: 1.0 confidence.
- News/Opinion/Media speculation: Max 0.6 confidence.

## Limitations
- Do not spend more than 3 rounds on a single query.
- Do not create more than 5 hypothesis pages per session unless instructed.
